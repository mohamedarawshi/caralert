CarAlert

Quickly alert nearby drivers by license plate. Private, anonymous, and easy to use.

App Store Google Play

How It Works

  • Search: Enter the car's plate number
  • Select: Choose a predefined alert (e.g. "Please move")
  • Send: The driver receives a notification, instantly

Why Use CarAlert?

  • No phone numbers or personal contact needed
  • Helps resolve parking and roadside issues fast
  • Get alerts when someone contacts you about your car
  • Daily alert limits to prevent spam
